About Me

I was born and raised in Birmingham and studied Mechanical Engineering at Sheffield university.

After uni I worked a few typical office jobs and spent a few some years in the energy industry. Despite my hard work I really felt like I was going nowhere and knew I needed a change of career. I've always been interested in technology and how things work, I love playing games and solving problems. After a bit of soul searching I decided to try my hand at building websites - a medium through which I can exercise my creativity and problem solving and find a challenging and satisfying career.

While I still have some distance to go before becoming a fully fledged web developer, I have never been more determined to make a success of myself. This is a brand new chapter in my life and I'm feeling excited and optimistic.
